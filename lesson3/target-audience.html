Target Audience

The target audience is people above 9 years old, who like to plan outdoor activities and that need a instrument to check the weather conditions. This will allow people to plan their activities depending on the weather conditions and wear the appropiate clothe.

Two Personas:

  • Medalia is a young mother. She likes to ride her bike every weekend. This weekly activity helps her to release stress and also provide the positive energy to face the hard work during the week. She likes to be surrounded by nature and breath pure air. That's why she love to check the weather forecast so she can plan her biking weekly activity.
  • Maximo is a 9 years old young man. He loves playing soccer every week. Most of the young man who attend to play soccer, love playing outdoor and in a green and big soccer filed yard.Maximo every week is wondering her mom how weather conditions would be for this week, so he could plan to go out and play soccer width his friedn. Having a site that would provide this forecast weather, will let him now and his friends, which is the best day to pick and play soccer.

Scenarios

  1. The sky is cloudy, and you never know when the rain will start. Could it be during the morning, evening or after noon?. To plan the next soccer match, it is essential to kknow the more accurate time when the rain will start.
  2. Too windy to ride the bike?. How long will be th wind blowing. What time will the wind stop blowing. Those question are so important to Medalia, so she could plan going out for a ride in her bike.
  3. And what about the temperature for today? Riding a bike or playing soccer, temperature is important so they can hydrate and provide the necessary water and protection to prevent from insolation.
  4. Is it cold or hot?. Should I use a jacket or can I wear my short pant?. Riding the bike or playing the soccer, the necessary outfit is important when doning sport. Depending on the weather condition, using different kind of outfit will provide better performance.
  5. What time the sun will rise? or what time is the sunset?. Considering the light time frame is important when riding the bike or playing soccer.
