Rule of Third

Gibson

gibson.com

Looking at the woman sitting on the right side, holding a guitar, it is evident that Rule of Third is applied to this picture. If we draw the crossed lines, we can observe how the majority of the woman human body right on the third quadrant and just on the cross, pointing the attention on that crossing point.

Contrast

Wendys

littlecaesars.com

The red color in contrast with white letters is one of the remarkable use of the Contrat principle in this picture. Most of the picture is in red color, the color of Wendys, so using the white color, highlight it and make the contrast more evident. In a less level, the yellow flames are contrasting with the white and red. It is also good to notice the brown nugget color contrasting with the white and red from the box.

Proximity

Nike

nike.com

White-letter are all closed each other, but also they are placed over the sneakers. This is not by mistake, It's the application of the principle of Proximity. Using this technique, Nike want to let the customer know the New Jordan Model are the sneakers that are under the logo, highlighting the beauty and perfection of it and also relating it to a famous basketball player.
