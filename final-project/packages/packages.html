Salmon River Adventures Rafting Time

Exploring

You can take a relaxing trip on the river, to enjoy its scenery and contemplate the beauty of nature.

Our tour allows you to take the raft, your camera and take the most excellent photos to remember. You can take the tour either alone, with your partner or family.

Our tour is designed so that children of any age can participate. The packages are also designed for companies and coworkers.

Rafting

Our kayak package includes up to 10 people. The trip lasts approximately 20 minutes. Includes all the necessary equipment and one of our instructors as a guide for your trip.

This package is designed for those who love adrenaline, have an exciting time and a lot, but a lot of water.

The travel route you can choose can be from low, medium and high rapids

Kayak

This is the most exciting adventure that we can offer you and that you can never have.

Traveling on the river in a kayak is the most adrenalinic package we can offer. This adventure is designed for experienced people, for those who love to descend alone and who have a knowledge of kayaking techniques.

The trip lasts approximately 20 minutes and you can choose the route according to the level of experience you have, whether in low, medium or high rapids.
